A hands-on tool to document your and your baby’s daily life during pregnancy.

The nine months prior to your child’s birth is a unique time in your life, as well as for your child indeed. Every moment of that period the baby is present, but it obviously can’t see nor remember where its parents are carrying him/her around during all those months.

How does it work?

1

fill in the form with information about your family. The more detailed the more personal the book will become.

2

Based on your uploaded information we design each spread of the book with a hand-written note about a possible situation in your daily life, as well as a drawing that illustrates that, in order to spire you.

3

we will send you the book + camera within approximately four weeks. You’re all set to start making your childs first photo album!
